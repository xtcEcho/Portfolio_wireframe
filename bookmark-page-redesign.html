THREE-D BOOKMARK PAGE

I designed and developed an experimental 3D web interface that convert a conventional “Bookmark” page into an interactive 3D space using THREE.JS.

CONCEPT

Getting to code with THREE.JS makes me realize how powerful and intuitive an interface can be when it’s presented in 3D, so I wanted to experiment and play with potentiality of space.

My goal is to recreate a straightforward and playful “bookmark” page to replace the conventional “bookmark" page, which is often presented by stacking our favorite websites on top of each other.

INSPIRATION

My inspiration came from James Turrell and Yayoi Kusama. These 2 artists utilized the nature of light and reflection and brought extraordinary impressions of space and dimensions to the viewers.

Yayoi Kusama

James Turrell

RECREATION

In order to fully emphasize on the immersive experience of 3d interface, I constructed a room with a mirror and floating blobs with mirror reflection.

Each blob represents a bookmarked website, and by turning the website handlers into objects, the experience of using bookmark page becomes way more intuitive and interesting.
